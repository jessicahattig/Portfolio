Jessica Hattig Software Developer

About

Currently enrolled in the full-stack software developer course at Epicodus and serving as an IT Coordinator at The Next Door, Inc., Jessica applies her proficiency in technology and software development to develop meaningful, outcome-driven solutions. Her unwavering commitment to leveraging technology for the betterment of society fuels her drive to make a positive impact.

Background

With a Bachelor of Science in Public Health, Jessica seamlessly integrates her expertise in environmentalism and community health to drive impactful change. Her extensive background as a sea kayak guide and ski instructor has honed her organizational and leadership skills, positioning her as a valuable asset to any team. During her tenure at Mt. Hood Meadows Ski Resort, she discovered her passion for harnessing technology to optimize business operations and champion people-oriented solutions.

Interests
